Production Unit

To ensure that we always deliver only quality products to clients, we have established a state-of-the-art production facility, where all manufacturing and finishing processes are carried out. At the CAD/CAM facility, our designers create sound designs of machines that can serve clients in a streamlined manner.


The engineers and technicians work in close coordination with each other and make use of the following machines to develop the range we offer:

  • Hydraulic and Power Press Machines
  • CNC Lathe Machines
  • CNC Milling Machines
  • CNC Shaping Machines
  • Rolling Machines
