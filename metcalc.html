HELP for weight calculator

You can freely use any combination of Imperial and Metric units.

Choose a material from the Material Selection Box.
Select the profile of the material- Sheet,Tube,Hex etc
Fill in the quantity or just leave the value at 1.
Fill in the dimensions of your material referring to the drawing at right-side of screen.
The value will be converted when you hit ENTER key or CALCULATE button.
The RESET button will change all the settings back to the start values.
Option to Select your preferred unit of Density from the Drop Down box.
