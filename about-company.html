Raj Industries Profile

Established in the year 1995 in Maharashtra, we, 'Raj Industries', Baramati, are engaged in manufacturing Textile & Industrial Machinery. Since inception, we have been involved in maintaining and servicing machines used in Textile Industry, and started fabricating substitutionary products for textile spares sometime later. The range of Textile Machinery offered by us comprises Roving Transport System for Ring Frame, Textile Spinning Floor Grating (Floor Silt), Carding Back Comb Assembly, Motor Driven Comber Drum Drive For Comber Machine. Currently, we offer quality machines that have revolutionized the way operations are carried out in textile industry, and lowered labour and overhead costs considerably. The textile machines developed by us are known for features like exceptional performance, long service lives, sturdy construction and reliability. Apart from Textile Machinery, we are also offering our clients Industrial Machinery which includes Industrial Batch Mixer, Grass Cutting Machine, Roller Conveyor, etc.

We have a sound infrastructure that is crucial for developing a quality range of Textile and Industrial Machinery. The standard and customized products are designed at the world class CAD/CAM facility and manufactured at the advanced production unit. The testing laboratory is used for quality control measures and research & development wing helps us in improving the range we offer. The warehousing & packaging unit is critical for efficiently packing and storing the products available with us. Due to the strategic placement of this facility, we are able to deliver clients' consignments in a timely manner.

We are headed by "Rajan Nair", a prominent entrepreneur in this line of work, who has guided us admirably. Under his aegis, we have become a force to be reckoned with in this highly competitive line of work, and garnered eminent clients, like Sri Sai Balaji Spintex (I) Pvt. Ltd., Sree Lalitha Parameswari Spg. Mills Pvt. Ltd. and Jyotirmaye Textiles.
